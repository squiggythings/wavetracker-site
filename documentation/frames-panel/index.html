Frames Panel


The frames panel is located at the top left of the main screen, below the module panel.

Frames are a sequence of patterns that describes the order of the song. During playback, the song will play through a frame one row at a time, then move on to the next frame in the sequence, and so on. Each square in the sequence represents one frame. The number inside the square is the pattern, the number above the square is the frame's order in the sequence.

The frame currently being edited will be highlighted in blue. Clicking the plus sign at the end of the frame list will add a new frame to the end of the song. Clicking a frame will navigate to editing that frame, and using the scroll wheel while hovered over this space will navigate forwards and backwards through frames.

Patterns can be reused across multiple frames for easy repetition. When creating a new frame, it will automatically hold the next empty pattern. To change a frame's pattern, hover over the frame, and either drag up and down, or hold shift and scroll up or down. A frame's pattern can also be set manually by right clicking the frame and selecting 'Set pattern...'

The small toolbar at the right allows simple functions for inserting a new frame, deleting the current frame, duplicating the current frame, and moving the current frame forwards / backwards in the sequence.
